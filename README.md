# JobSculptor 💼

> Refine your resume and match your skills with the best jobs and internships using AI.

[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](LICENSE)

## 🎯 Overview

JobSculptor is an intelligent job discovery and resume optimization platform. It uses AI to analyze your resume against job descriptions, provides actionable improvement suggestions, and matches you with opportunities that align with your skills and career goals.

**Stop applying blindly. Start applying strategically.**

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔍 Skill-Based Job Search
- Input your skills, interests, and career preferences
- AI-powered job discovery
- Filter by location, experience level, and more

### 📄 Resume Analysis & Optimization
- Upload your resume (PDF or DOCX)
- AI compares your resume against job descriptions
- Get actionable suggestions for improvements

### 📊 Intelligent Job Matching
- Personalized match scores for each job (0-100%)
- Breakdown of matching criteria
- Visual match indicators

### 🔖 Application Tracking
- Bookmark jobs for later review
- Track your applications with custom statuses
- Add notes and follow-up dates
